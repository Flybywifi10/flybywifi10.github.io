Redlands, California Temple

  • Announcement: 21 April 2001
  • Groundbreaking and Site Dedication: 1 December 2001 by Dieter F. Uchtdorf
  • Dedication: 14 September 2003 by Gordon B. Hinckley
  • Architectural Features: Single attached spire with an angel Moroni statue
  • Site: 4.6 acres
  • Total Floor Area: 17,300 square feet
  • The Redlands California Temple sits on the Church's original landholdings in the San Bernardino colony, established in 1851 under the direction of Brigham Young.
